07 — Brand Voice
How We
Write
Positioning
AI adoption that doesn't break production systems. Write like a senior engineer sharing field notes — not a thought leader selling a framework.
We are
Sharp, direct, practitioner-level. Practical takes on AI adoption, real engineering decisions, and what's actually working in the field. Written for engineering leaders who are already building — not being sold to. Lead with something they can use on Monday.
We're not
Academic, trendy, or vague. No fluff, no frameworks dressed up as insight. Never talk down to the room — assume the reader already knows what they're doing.
We are
  • Sharp
  • Direct
  • Practitioner-level
  • Field-tested
We're not
  • Academic
  • Trendy
  • Vague
  • Salesy
Tone check

Before posting, ask: "Would a senior engineer find something useful here by Monday?" If yes, it's on-brand.
